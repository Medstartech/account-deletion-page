Account Deletion - vacuactivus_user

This page explains how to request account deletion for the vacuactivus_user app, developed by Medstar Tech.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the vacuactivus_user app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your request.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Account credentials (email, phone number, etc.)
  • Training and session history

What data is retained

We do not retain any personal data after account deletion. However, anonymized app usage statistics may be preserved for analytics and crash reporting purposes.

How long it takes

Your account and associated data will be deleted within 2 business days from the time of your request.
